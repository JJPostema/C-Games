`agario.cpp`: a simple Agar.io-like minigame where you must eat foods to grow.

CONTROLS:

CLICK - Move to that location

SPACE (when single) - Split

SPACE (when double) - Merge

`planet-simulator.cpp`: a simple gravitation simulator.

CONTROLS:

LEFT CLICK - Create a small planet

RIGHT CLICK - Create a massive planet

CLICK AND DRAG - Give initial momentum to a planet

SCROLL - Double the gravitational constant
